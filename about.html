We believe Thailand deserves better than mass tourism.

EcoExplorer Thailand was born from frustration with exploitative travel operators and greenwashed “eco-tours.” We are a small independent team of travelers, conservationists and Thai locals who want you to experience the real Thailand — the one that still has wild elephants, healthy reefs, and vibrant communities.

Our promise

  • ✅
    No paid promotions. We never accept free trips or affiliate kickbacks from operators that harm animals or nature.
  • ✅
    Full transparency. Every recommendation includes real trade-offs, costs, and the exact impact on local ecosystems and people.
  • ✅
    Local voices first. Every major guide is reviewed by the Thai families and rangers who actually live and work in these places.

Who we are

Founded in 2024 by Mae (Thai conservationist) and Alex (slow-travel photographer). We have spent more than 8 combined years exploring every corner of Thailand by motorbike, longtail boat, and on foot — always with a zero-waste mindset and a refusal to support any form of animal exploitation.

🐘

We have visited 19 elephant sanctuaries. Only 4 met our strict ethical standards.

🌊

We have dived and snorkeled 27 reefs. We only recommend the 9 that are actively protected by local communities.
